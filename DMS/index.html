Frequently Asked Questions About Route Optimization Software

What is route optimization software?

Route optimization software helps businesses plan the most efficient routes for delivery vehicles. It uses algorithms and real-time data to minimize drive time, avoid traffic delays, reduce fuel costs, and increase the number of stops per day. Modern route optimization software can reduce delivery costs by up to 40% and cut planning time by 95%.

How is RouteFlo different from Routific or OptimoRoute?

RouteFlo is built specifically for recurring service delivery - mat rental, linen services, and uniform delivery. Unlike generic route software designed for one-time deliveries, RouteFlo handles customers with set service days (Mon/Wed/Fri, weekly, bi-weekly) and generates routes that follow your existing service patterns. It includes materials tracking that general delivery software lacks.

How much time does route planning software save?

Route planning software typically reduces manual planning time by 95%. What used to take hours of spreadsheet work can be done in seconds. Operations also see 15-40% reductions in total drive time through optimized routing, saving on fuel costs and allowing more deliveries per day.

How long does it take to set up?

Most operations are up and running within a day. You can import your existing customer list via CSV, set up your drivers, and start generating optimized routes immediately. No special hardware or app store downloads required.

Do drivers need a special app?

Drivers access their schedule through a simple web app that works on any smartphone. One tap opens each stop in Google Maps for turn-by-turn navigation. No app store download required - just open a link in the browser.

What industries use route optimization software?

Route optimization software is used across delivery industries including mat rental, linen services, uniform delivery, food distribution, HVAC services, and any business with recurring route-based operations. RouteFlo specializes in textile rental and recurring service delivery.
