# TOOL #1: The Email Assistant
## Problem: Spending 2-3 hours per day drowning in emails

### What It Fixes
- Inbox overload
- Repetitive responses to similar questions
- Time spent crafting professional replies
- Following up on leads/clients

### The Tool: ChatGPT + Email Templates System

**Cost:** Free (ChatGPT free tier) or $20/month (ChatGPT Plus for faster responses)

**Time to Set Up:** 15 minutes

**Time Saved:** 10-15 hours per week

---

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Step 1: Create Your Email Response Library**

Open ChatGPT and use this prompt:

```
I need to create a library of email templates for my business.

My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS IN 1 SENTENCE]

I frequently get emails asking about:
1. [COMMON QUESTION 1]
2. [COMMON QUESTION 2]
3. [COMMON QUESTION 3]
4. [COMMON QUESTION 4]
5. [COMMON QUESTION 5]

For each of these, write a professional but friendly email response template.
Keep them under 100 words. Match my tone: [professional/casual/direct/warm].

Include placeholders for [NAME] and [SPECIFIC DETAIL] that I can customize.
```

**Step 2: Save Your Templates**

Copy the templates ChatGPT generates into:
- A Google Doc (easiest)
- Text expansion tool (TextExpander, Phrase Express)
- Email snippets/canned responses (Gmail, Outlook)

**Step 3: Create an AI Email Drafter**

When you get a unique email that needs a thoughtful response, use this prompt:

```
Write a professional email response to this:

[PASTE THE EMAIL YOU RECEIVED]

Context about my business: [1-2 SENTENCES]

Tone: [professional/friendly/direct/warm]

Keep it under 150 words. Be helpful and clear.
```

**Step 4: The Follow-Up Helper**

Use this prompt for follow-ups:

```
I need to follow up with someone I haven't heard back from.

Original context: [WHAT YOU'RE FOLLOWING UP ABOUT]

Last contact: [WHEN YOU LAST REACHED OUT]

Write a brief, professional follow-up email that:
- Isn't pushy
- Adds value or a helpful reminder
- Makes it easy for them to respond

Under 75 words.
```

### Step-by-Step Setup

**Step 1: Export Your Messy Data**
- Export from your CRM, email list, or wherever data lives
- Save as CSV or copy into Google Sheets

**Step 2: Use ChatGPT to Write Formulas**

Instead of googling "how to remove duplicates in Google Sheets," ask ChatGPT:

```
I have a Google Sheet with customer data in columns A-E.

Column A: Names (some are "John Smith", some are "Smith, John", inconsistent)
Column B: Email addresses (some duplicates)
Column C: Phone numbers (some formatted (555) 123-4567, some 555-123-4567)

I need:
1. A formula to standardize all names to "First Last" format
2. A way to identify duplicate emails
3. A formula to format all phone numbers consistently as (XXX) XXX-XXXX

Give me step-by-step instructions with the exact formulas to use.
```

ChatGPT will give you the exact formulas, where to put them, and how to use them.

**Step 3: Clean and Categorize Data**

```
I have a list of 500 customer comments/feedback in column A of a Google Sheet.

I need to categorize them into:
- Product feedback
- Customer service issues
- Feature requests
- General compliments
- Complaints

How can I use AI or formulas to automatically categorize these instead of reading all 500 manually?
```

ChatGPT will suggest approaches (formulas, scripts, or even offer to categorize if you paste a sample).

**Step 4: Extract Insights**

```
Here's my sales data from last quarter [PASTE DATA or describe it]:

Analyze this and tell me:
1. What are the top 3 patterns or trends?
2. What's surprising or unexpected?
3. What should I focus on based on this data?
4. What questions should I be asking that I'm not?
```
