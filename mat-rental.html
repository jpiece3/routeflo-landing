Mat Rental Route Software FAQ

What is mat rental route software?

Mat rental route software is specialized route optimization designed for floor mat delivery companies. It handles the unique needs of recurring mat service - managing customer service schedules (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly), tracking mat inventory at each location, and generating efficient routes that respect established service patterns.

How does mat rental route optimization work?

Mat rental route optimization takes your customer list with their service schedules and generates efficient routes for each service day. The software considers stop locations, time windows, driver availability, and recurring patterns to build routes that minimize drive time while ensuring every customer gets serviced on their scheduled day.

Can I track floor mat inventory at each location?

Yes. RouteFlo includes mat inventory tracking that shows what mats are assigned to each customer location, what's due for pickup, and what needs to be delivered. Drivers see this information in their mobile app.

How is this different from generic route planning software?

Generic route planning software is built for one-time deliveries. Mat rental operations are fundamentally different: you visit the same customers on set schedules, need to maintain route consistency, and must track what's being picked up and dropped off. RouteFlo is built specifically for this recurring service model.

How long does it take to set up?

Most mat rental operations are up and running within a day. Import your customer list via CSV, set up service schedules, and start generating optimized routes immediately.
